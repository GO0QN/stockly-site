12-Week Stockly Options Course

A day-by-day guided progression, inspired by adaptive learning flows.

Week 1: Market BasicsBid-ask • Orders • Candlesticks • VWAP
  • Day 1–2: What is a stock? Bid-ask, spreads, order types.
  • Day 3–4: Candlesticks & chart patterns (support/resistance).
  • Day 5–6: Liquidity, volume, VWAP basics.
  • Day 7: Quiz + practice trading in simulator.
Week 2: Trading Psychology & RiskFOMO • Revenge trading • Sizing
  • Daily lessons on FOMO, revenge trading, stop-loss placement.
  • Risk/reward ratios (2:1, 3:1), position sizing.
  • End-week simulation: manage a $10k account for one “day.”
Week 3: Options FoundationsCalls • Puts • Chains • Delta/Theta
  • Calls vs. puts explained.
  • Option chain structure (strike, expiry, premium).
  • Basic Greeks (Delta & Theta).
  • End-week test: choose the right contract per scenario.
Week 4: Intermediate OptionsVega • Gamma • IV crush • Assignment
  • Vega & Gamma explained.
  • Implied Volatility & IV crush.
  • Assignment & exercise risk.
  • Match-game: connect market scenarios to strategies.
Week 5: Income Strategies ICovered calls • Cash-secured puts
  • Covered calls, cash-secured puts.
  • When to sell premium.
  • AI Coach review: auto-generate a trade journal.
Week 6: Income Strategies IICredit & Debit Spreads
  • Credit spreads (bull put, bear call).
  • Debit spreads (bull call, bear put).
  • Practice exam: manage a spread portfolio for a simulated week.
Week 7: Advanced Strategies IStraddles • Strangles • Earnings
  • Straddles, strangles (earnings plays).
  • Risk of high IV.
  • Challenge: pick best strategy given an earnings calendar.
Week 8: Advanced Strategies IICondors • Butterflies • Calendars
  • Condors, butterflies.
  • Calendar spreads.
  • AI Tutor: build each spread in Options Lab, then test scenarios.
Week 9: Day Trading with OptionsScalp vs Swing • ORB • VWAP • Liquidity
  • Scalping vs. swinging options.
  • Intraday setups (ORB, VWAP, liquidity sweeps).
  • Live Battle: timed simulation against peers.
Week 10: Risk Mastery & HedgingProtective Puts • Portfolio Greeks
  • Using puts as protection.
  • Portfolio Greeks.
  • Simulation: hedge a $50k virtual equity portfolio with options.
Week 11: Strategy IntegrationStocks + Options • Journaling
  • Blend equity trades with options structures.
  • Journaling every trade (AI Coach auto-reviews).
  • Quiz/test: pick strategies under time constraints.
Week 12: Final Exam (Capstone)$100k Paper Account • Full Week
  • Trade a full simulated week ($100k paper account).
  • Apply strategies, risk management, and journaling.
  • AI Coach + Mentor Dashboard deliver final evaluation.
